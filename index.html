Bookmarklets

View the Project on GitHub svenmueller/Bookmarklets

Installing a bookmarklet is easy:

  1. First, make sure your browser's bookmark toolbar is visible. In Safari, choose "Show Bookmarks Bar" from the View menu.
  2. Second, drag the bookmarklet button to the right to your Bookmarks Toolbar.
  3. That's it!
